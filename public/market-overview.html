Market Overview

The global healthcare data analytics market size was valued at $23.8 billion in 2021 and is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 20.4% from 2021 to 2028. The blockchain healthcare market is expected to grow from $176 million in 2020 to $546 million by 2025.

Data Security

Healthcare data contains sensitive information, making security and privacy paramount. Recent data breaches highlight the need for secure data storage and management.

Blockchain technology ensures data integrity, decentralized storage, and transparent and traceable access records, enhancing data security.

Target Users

Our platform is designed for:

  • Individual users who want to manage and securely share their health data.
  • Healthcare providers who need accurate and comprehensive patient data for diagnosis and treatment.
  • Clinical researchers who require access to health data for research purposes.

Token Economy

Our token economy incentivizes data sharing and activity on the platform. Tokens are distributed as follows:

  • Total supply: 1,000,000,000 PHT
  • Maximum supply: 1,500,000,000 PHT
  • Initial distribution:
    • Users: 30% (300,000,000 PHT)
    • Healthcare providers: 25% (250,000,000 PHT)
    • Researchers: 20% (200,000,000 PHT)
    • Project team: 15% (150,000,000 PHT)
  • Annual additional issuance: Up to 5% to support ecosystem expansion, subject to DAO voting.
